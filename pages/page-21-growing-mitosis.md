## Goal of This Page

Mitosis — the "boring" type of cell division. TWO concepts: (1) Mitosis is how your body grows and repairs — one cell becomes two IDENTICAL daughter cells, each with the full 46 chromosomes, and (2) The key limitation — mitosis produces CLONES, not variety. It maintains your genome but doesn't create anything new. This matters because it sets up the contrast with meiosis, which DOES create variety.
